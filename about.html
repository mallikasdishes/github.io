About

I'm Mallika, and welcome to my website! I love to cook & try out new recipes. I will be sharing new recipes from time to time on my website here. If you enjoy any of the recipes I post, feel free to leave a comment.

I grew up in South India, and learned to cook from my mother, relatives and friends. I am a full-time mom, and always trying out new things to keep my hungry kids happy :o). Some of these recipes have been passed onto me from many generations, and if you are into authentic South Indian Vegetarian food, you've come to the right place.

If you have any specific questions or want to request for a new recipe, please feel free to drop me a message. Happy Cooking!
