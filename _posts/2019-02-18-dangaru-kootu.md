It has a bit sourness and spicy taste to it, and is a bit sticky compared to other yogurt based dishes due to the addition of Urad Dal Powder.

      * Limes - 2 Count
      * Turmeric - 1/2 Spoon
      * Curry Leaves - 1 Bunch
